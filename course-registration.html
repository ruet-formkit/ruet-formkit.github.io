Instructions
About

A specialized web app for RUET students to generate course registration forms in the official university format with smart autocomplete, auto-load, and PDF download.

How to Use
  1. Fill Student Information — Department, Roll, Registration No., Name, Session, and Previous Credit.
  2. Auto-load Courses — Select dept & semester then click Load Courses.
  3. Edit Manually — Click Add Course and use the autocomplete search.
  4. Download PDF — Click Download PDF to save the official RUET LEGAL-size form.
