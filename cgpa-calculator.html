📖 How to Use
  1. 1
    Select Department & Semester
    In the Auto-load bar at the top of the Current Semester card, choose your department and semester from the dropdowns, then click ⚡ Load Courses to populate the course list automatically.
  2. 2
    Choose Input Mode
    Switch between Marks (%) and Letter Grade mode using the toggle. In Marks mode enter your percentage (0–100); in Grade mode pick a letter grade from the dropdown.
  3. 3
    Add / Edit Courses Manually
    Click ＋ Add Course to insert a new row. Search by course name or code using the combo inputs. Credits are filled automatically when a course is selected.
  4. 4
    Calculate SGPA
    Click Calculate SGPA → to see your Semester GPA instantly. The result and letter grade appear in the result strip below.
  5. 5
    Calculate CGPA
    In the CGPA Calculator card, either enter individual semester SGPAs using By Semester SGPA mode, or enter your previous CGPA & total credits using Overall CGPA + Credits mode. The current SGPA (if calculated) is included automatically.
  6. 6
    View CGPA Result
    Your final CGPA with the animated ring visual, letter grade, total credits, and semester breakdown appears in the result card.
